TOKYO AP The European champion Real Madrid of Spain beat South American counterpart Vasco Da Gama of Brazil 2-1 Tuesday night in a game to decide the world's No. 1 soccer club team. Real Madrid led 1-0 at halftime and stayed ahead throughout the remainder of the game before more than 51000 spectators at Japan's National Stadium in downtown Tokyo.
