JERUSALEM AP The political consultant who engineered Bill Clinton's come-from-behind victory in the 1992 presidential race hopes to do the same for the Israeli opposition leader. Campaign adviser James Carville will arrive in Israel next month to talk strategy with Ehud Barak a former armed forces chief of staff and leader of the opposition Labor Party. Barak has failed to pull ahead in the polls despite the repeated scandals that have tarnished Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu.
