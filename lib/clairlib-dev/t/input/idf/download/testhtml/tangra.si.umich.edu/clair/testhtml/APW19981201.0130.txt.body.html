MOSCOW AP Prime Minister Yevgeny Primakov will visit India later this month for talks on political relations and trade the government said Tuesday. Primakov's visit will replace a trip by Boris Yeltsin that was canceled in November because of the Russian president's poor health. Primakov will visit India Dec. 20-23 but no details were provided on his itinerary.
