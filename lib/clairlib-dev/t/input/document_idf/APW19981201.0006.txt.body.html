MELBOURNE Australia AP A helicopter rescue team on Tuesday reached a three-man television crew that had been stranded on a remote Arctic island for more than two weeks rescuers said. The helicopter sent by international medical emergency company AEA International SOS landed on frozen Wrangel Island at about 0200 GMT said company spokesman Mark Crawford.
