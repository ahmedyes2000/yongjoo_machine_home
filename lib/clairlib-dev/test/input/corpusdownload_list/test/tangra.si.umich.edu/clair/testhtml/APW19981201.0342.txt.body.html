ADELAIDE Australia AP The South Australian government will explore opportunities in the Philippines that could involve water management in provincial cities South Australian state Premier John Olsen said Tuesday. Olsen who met with former Philippines president Fidel Ramos said water management was a key focus for the South Australian government and one in which it had the expertise to help the Philippines. ``There are 15 cities with a population of 1 million people in the Philippines -- and Mr. Ramos today indicated to me that there are opportunities for South Australia to become involved in water management in those areas'' Olsen said.
