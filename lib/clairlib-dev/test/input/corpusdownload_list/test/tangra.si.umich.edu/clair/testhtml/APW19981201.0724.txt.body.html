BERLIN AP Shirley MacLaine who won an Oscar for her starring role in ``Terms of Endearment'' will receive the Golden Bear award for lifetime achievement at the 49th Berlin film festival organizers announced Tuesday. Film festival director Moritz de Hadeln hailed MacLaine's ``extraordinary versatility'' and said she embodies Hollywood glamour. MacLaine 64 will receive her award Feb. 18.
