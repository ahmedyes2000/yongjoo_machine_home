   WARSAW, Feb 15 (AFP) - Polish Prime Minister Jerzy Buzek is due  
to visit Sweden on Tuesday and Wednesday to meet his Swedish 
counterpart Goeran Persson and parliamentary speaker Birgitt Dahl, 
his press service said Monday. 
   Buzek will also meet Swedish Conservative opposition leader Carl  
Bildt. 
   He is expected to discuss bilateral economic and regional  
cooperation as well as European integration, the press service 
said.
