   LIMA, Feb 14 (AFP) - The French tourism and hotel group Accor is  
considering investing upwards of 80 million dollars in Peru, company 
officials said here Sunday. 
   Accor representatives are to meet with a Peruvian company Friday  
to discuss plans to create the Cuzco Hotel corporation, an eight 
million dollar investment. 
   The corporation plans to buy a colonial mansion in the ancient  
imperial city of Cuzco and turn it into a five-star hotel. 
   According to the Peruvian ministry of industry and tourism,  
Accor operates in 142 countries, running more than 2,800 hotels of 
varying categories.
